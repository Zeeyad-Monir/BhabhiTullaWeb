Bhabhi Tulla Rules

Objective: Be the first to get rid of all the cards in your hand. The last player with cards is the "Bhabhi" (loser).

Setup: The game uses a standard 52-card deck. Each player is dealt an equal number of cards.

Gameplay:

  • The player holding the Ace of Spades starts the first trick.
  • Players must follow the suit of the lead card if possible.
  • If you have cards of the same suit, you must play a higher card than the lead card if possible.
  • If you can't play a higher card of the same suit, you can play a lower card of the same suit.
  • If you don't have any cards of the lead suit, you can play any card (it won't win the trick).
  • The highest card of the lead suit wins the trick.
  • The winner of the trick leads the next trick.

Card Rankings: Ace (high) > King > Queen > Jack > 10 > 9 > ... > 2

Winning: The first player to get rid of all their cards wins. The last player with cards is the "Bhabhi" (loser).
